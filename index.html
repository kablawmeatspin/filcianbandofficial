About the band

FilciaN is a multi-genre experimental music band that was formed in 2013.
The band was active for the few months last 2013. One year later the band were became inactive
for 11 years that was because of their financial problem. But the band were not losing hope even
it's been years inactive they still encouraged and motivated. The good thing is, the time has come
for them to arise and now they return in 2024.
